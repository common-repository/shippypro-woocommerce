ShippyPro Plugin for WooCommerce. Generate orders labels for more than 50+ carriers and connect more than 15+ marketplaces with just one single platform, Displays Live Shipping Rates based on the Shipping Address and Cart Content.

== Description ==

ShippyPro is a cloud shipping software that allows you to integrate multiple carriers with one click.

Show your carriers fares and dropoff points inside your WooCommerce checkout with the ShippyPro's plugin!

With ShippyPro plugin your WooCommerce checkout will show live carriers fares for each order and your customers will be able to choose their favourite carrier and additional options such as Dropoff Locations.

You and your customers will not overpay anymore for a remote area surcharge delivery!</b>

<b>The extension requires a free ShippyPro account. Sign up at shippypro.com</b>

With ShippyPro you can create shipping labels in bulk, track packages and manage shipments like a pro, saving time and money on shipping

You will be charged in addition only if you want to use ShippyPro to create shipping label faster, connecting all your selling platforms.

When you create a shipping label in ShippyPro, the system will show you the shipping options chosen by your customer at the checkout

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins' directory.

2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

3. Thats it, you can now configure the plugin.
